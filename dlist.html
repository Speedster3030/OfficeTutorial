Using Description Lists

Soliloquy
In drama, where, a character speaks to themselves, represemting their inner thoughts and feelings and in the process relaying them to the audience (but not to other characters).
Monologue
In a drama when a character speaks their thoughts out loud to share them with the audience and any characters present.
Aside
In drama, where the character shares a comment with the audience for humurous or drmamtic effect.
